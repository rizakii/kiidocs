--- layout: rest-api title: 'Template name' tags: rest-api ---
Resource for template name. The template names involves the content to manage:
  • email-verified.body : Template for the content body of email verified
  • email-verified.subject : Template for the subject of email verified
  • email-verification-needed.body : Template for the content body of a verification email required
  • email-verification-needed.subject : Template for the subject of a verification email required
  • password-reset-request.body : Template for the content body of a password reset request
  • password-reset-request.subject : Template for the subject of a password reset request
  • password-reset-completed.body : Template for the content body of a completed operation of password reset
  • password-reset-completed.subject : Template for the subject of a completed operation of password reset
Location/apps/{appID}/templates/{templateName}
